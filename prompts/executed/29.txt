# Leira v1.18 — Verification Record

## Mission

Implement deterministic Verification Record support.

This slice records independently observed verification evidence for an Implementation Report.

It does not approve, reject, dispatch, execute, or decide what happens next.

It merely preserves what was checked and what was observed.
