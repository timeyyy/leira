# Leira v3.2 — Human Decision Commit

## Mission

Implement the deterministic **Human Decision Commit**.

This is **not** a planner.

This is **not** a dispatcher.

This is **not** autonomous.

Its sole responsibility is to convert an explicitly approved HumanDecisionDraft into a HumanDecisionRecord.

The commit is explicitly invoked by a human.

Without an explicit commit request, nothing happens.

---

## Files

Create:

```text
leira/human_decision_commit/
    __init__.py
    commit.py
    test_human_decision_commit.py
```

Modify nothing else unless strictly necessary.
